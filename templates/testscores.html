Welcome to the Test Scores Page!

Here you can view of how decent ACT/SAT scores should be in order to go to certain colleges. Many reputable colleges will only admit students with high ACT or SAT test scores. Many colleges are test-optional meaning that a student does not need to take the ACT, nor the SAT to get admitted to a certain college. Having ACT/SAT scores can help you qualify for certain scholarships when entering colleges as a student fresh out from high school (age <21).

ACT/SAT Score Ranges

  • ACT Score of 1-19 (SAT Score of <1010): Below Average (Poor)
  • ACT Score of 20-25 (SAT Score of 1020 - 1230): Average (Good)
  • ACT Score of 26-29 (SAT Score of 1240 - 1380): Above Average (Competitive)
  • ACT Score of 30-35 (SAT Score of 1390 - 1590): Excellent (Best)
  • ACT Score of 36 (SAT Score of 1600): Perfect (Maximum)
