Welcome to the Majors Page!

Here you can view a list of majors by career field. Certain majors like engineering and STEM-related majors will require more work than other majors. It is important to consider what you are interested in, what career pathway you want to go to, how much income you want to make, and whether you want to pursue furthur education after college. Also, don't rely on your mentors and parents only when considering a major. Note: The majors came directly from the Kansas State University and the University of Kansas websites.
